[Redesigning the toothbrush]



Type :

BEng Individual Project

Time :

2018/2019

Location :

University of Surrey




(01)
ABOUT THE PROJECT

    This project is based on designing an oral cleansing tool to rival the current toothbrushes in the market. The final product designed is a sonic toothbrush made of polypropylene, which utilizes a silicone brush head and unlike the conventional bristles has a semi-circular pattern.

(02)
THE PROBLEM

    Conventional toothbrushes have a lot of obvious disadvantages, mostly relating to their use of Nylon bristles and since they are used daily, it is crucial for them to be as effective as possible. It has been shown that bristle wear and contamination, seriously reduce their efficacy of brushing and in conventional toothbrushes this occurs already within a few months of use (Tangade, 2013; Frazelle, 2012).

    Such factors were considered during the design process, to make the product more effective, while also taking into a count the user’s convenience. when an unknown printer took a galley of type and scrambled.
