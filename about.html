about me.


Currently working with Deluxesign3D using Robotic 3D printing via Fused Granular Fabrication (FGF) to manufacture large aesthetical and functional projects.

Georgios Goudelis strives to create things that he believes should exist in this world and design is the best medium for him to transform his visions into a reality. Without a doubt, design has the capability to transform the world both physically and emotionally. However, he believes that it is necessary for one to have control of the technology behind design, to be able to push and break the boundaries of creativity.

He has graduated with two masters, one in Mechanical Engineering from UCL and one in Industrial Design from Politecnico di Milano. This has influenced his approach in design to combine both technical and creative methods. The challenge in today's complex world, is applying the right balance between the two, depending on each project at hand. His experience in both scientific and creative environments offers him a unique perspective on this, having worked in high-tech industry, design studio and digital fabrication labs. Moreover, as a believer in pushing boundaries, he has invested a time in the research field and has also successfully published scientific papers. His constant curiosity and experiences from living in 5 different countries, has offered him exposure to a multitude of cultures, from which he has learnt, drawn inspiration and today bases his creative intuition.
