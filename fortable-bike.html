[E-sit Foldable Bike]


Client :

Bianchi



Time :

08/22 - 01/23



Description :

This was a university group project, which aimed at
designing an electric foldable bike for Bianchi,
with a commuting context. Respecting the company's
constraints, the solution offered is a bike that
allows the user to comfortably sit on it when folded
without compromising its ease of use and intuitiveness.
